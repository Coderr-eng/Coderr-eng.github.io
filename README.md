📌 Project Description

Shared Hearth is a cozy digital space designed for long-distance couples to stay emotionally connected. It allows partners to share memories, vent feelings, generate date ideas, and send emotional nudges — all within a private shared environment.

❓ The Problem Statement

Long-distance relationships often suffer from emotional distance, lack of spontaneous interactions, and limited shared experiences. Traditional messaging apps do not provide a dedicated emotional space for couples to express feelings, store memories, and plan meaningful virtual moments.

💡 The Solution

Shared Hearth provides a centralized emotional hub where couples can:

Share memories in a scrapbook-style diary

Express emotions in a safe vent space

Generate creative virtual date ideas

Send subtle “glow” nudges to feel connected

It creates intimacy through design, simplicity, and interactive features.
